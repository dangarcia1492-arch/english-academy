Asking the way.

5 questions ~6 minutes +20 XP
The scene

You're in London, looking for King's Cross. You see someone friendly. Here's how to ask the British way — politely, without sounding lost or like a tourist.

Listen first
You Excuse me, sorry — could you tell me how to get to King's Cross?
A Londoner Oh yeah, just go straight, then take the second left. It's a five-minute walk.
You Brilliant, thanks so much.
Key phrases
Excuse me, sorry polite interruption — never just "perdón"
Could you tell me how to… ¿Podrías decirme cómo…?
Brilliant great / cool — very British
Take the second left la segunda calle a la izquierda
